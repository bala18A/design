About SRIMAK


Sri Mak has been specializing in providing quality Printer repair and support services in the Chennai land area for many years. Supplying consumable parts for all range of Printer-Plotter and providing genuine spare parts. We also provide Rental Solutions on a short and long term basis with complete maintenance support.


We are pleased to introduce ourselves as sale and Service providers for all range of Printers –Plotter –Copier machines – Desktop and Laptops. We also distributing product like Consumable Parts and Hardware in all branded printers and plotters. We also deal in computer peripherals and products like Projectors / CCTV / Security Systems and Telecommunication.


Our services have certainly helped us not only in winning good clientele but also share a long term relationship with them. An emphasis on looking beyond the obvious and finding more cost-efficient and time-saving solutions alongside this growth


Providing after sales and support has been appreciated by all our customers and probably it is the single most reason for our continuous growth
